New Dallara P217 Livery

Follow along here on the Apex Tech Racing official website and social channels to see the car in action in these and many other races.

November 22, 2020

NASA Feature Race Round 3 Results!

Apex Tech Racing is pleased to announce Team Meridian driver Kamal Hashimee finished in 5th place in the most recent round of the NASA GT Americas Presented By Spark GTE Championship at Lime Rock Park. This result moves Hashimee to 12th position in the championship. Other strong race finishes have positioned team drivers Drew Williams in 18th place, EJ Feulner in 24th place, Lee Tucker in 25th place and Chris Pio in 28th position. And team TPI driver EJ Feulner remains in second place in the ATK Liveries Hard Charger Championship representing the driver with the most positions gained over all races.

October 27, 2020

Apex Technologies USA announces the creation
of Apex Tech Racing Sim Racing Team
- EJ Feulner

Along with its top manufacturer partners, Apex Technologies USA is proud to announce the creation of a high level iRacing Virtual Sim Road Racing Team. Apex Tech Racing will compete in the top Road Racing Prototype and GT leagues, events and series on the iRacing platform. With a roster of accomplished iRacing road racers hailing from across the United States, Apex Tech Racing will compete in the newly formed NASA GT Americas presented By Spark GTE Series in the Chevrolet C8R Corvette, the Virtual Endurance Championship in the Dallara P217 LMP2 Prototype, iRacing Special Endurance Events in multiple cars and many individual endurance races and charity events over the 2020 and 2021 iRacing seasons.

Founded during the height of the COVID pandemic by Apex Technologies CEO and first Apex Tech Racing Team Principal Ryan Donaher who was able to reach out to like minded sim racers he was competing with on a daily and weekly basis to put this group of veteran racers together. He explains, “I decided early on if we were going to do this, we were going to do it right. Our most important tenet on this team is to have fun. If we can compete with the best sim racers while having fun, then that is a win for us.” But make no mistake Donaher and the rest of team expects to battle and collect checkered flags. He continues “We intend to win. Compete and win. Period. Our drivers and our sponsors expect nothing less.”

And speaking of sponsors, Apex Technologies USA is uniquely positioned to partner with the best in class technology manufacturers and brands it represents in the United States to make this team even stronger. Primary sponsor All In Sports (AIS)- the leading racing simulator manufacturer led by race winning Formula 1 Race Engineers- is thrilled to be a part of the team. As AIS owner and previous head of Ferrari F1 Simulator Department Anton Stipinovich relates “We have been building the best racing simulators in the world for more than a decade and a half. Every day we use technology we developed in Formula 1 to offer our clients the best virtual racing experiences available anywhere. With Apex Technologies firmly representing and growing our footprint in the United States, it was natural for us to be involved with the new sim racing team. Top Formula 1 drivers like Fernando Alonso and Charles Leclerc use our simulators and race with AIS logos proudly displayed on their virtual cars. Rest assured the guys on Apex Tech Racing are backed by the best in the business.”

With associate sponsors Meridian Audio and TPI Cinema also fully supporting the team and its drivers Apex Tech Racing is poised to make a huge splash on the iRacing platform. Donaher exclaims “We are extremely lucky to have such like minded business partners. They firmly believe in this race team and who knows- maybe someday we can go from virtual to real life racing with the same crew of sponsors and drivers?”
